I have garnered a lot of experience in learning about mindfulness and how it plays a role in our day-to-day life and how we as humans can further improve our mindfulness. I have also studied the state of flow in the psychological sense. Being in a state of flow means that you are so focused on something to the point where you lose track of what is happening around you. This usually happens with activities that are mildly tedious, but not too tedious where it is overwhelming. For example, people can get lost in flow when they are playing a video game or even when they are playing an instrument. The research I have conducted has mainly been focused on mindfulness and flow.
